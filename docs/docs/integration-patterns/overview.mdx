Supaglue supports three main integration patterns to interact with your customers' SaaS tools (aka third-party [Providers](../category/providers)):

1. [**Managed Syncs**](./managed-syncs): Sync data from your customers' third-party providers into your infrastructure.
1. [**Unified API**](./unified-api): Read and write to multiple third-party providers with a single API.
1. [**Real-time Events**](./real-time-events): Receive real-time notifications when data changes in your customers' third-party providers.
